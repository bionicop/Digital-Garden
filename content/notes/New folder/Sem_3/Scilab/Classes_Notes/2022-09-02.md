### Area of Triangle -

*Input-*
```scilab
//Area of Triangle
side1= input("side 1- ");
side2 = input("side 2- ");
side3 = input("side 3- ");

x=(side1 + side2 + side3)/2;
area=sqrt((x)*(x-side1)*(x-side2)*(x-side3));
disp("Area of Triangle: ", area);
```

*Output-*
```scilab
side 1- 3

side 2- 4

side 3- 5


  "Area of Triangle: "

   6.
```
